Homework 7: Census data choropleth

Logan Timm

I chose to look at data from Virginia because I grew up and went to school in southern VA. What I really wanted to investigate was public education funding by county because schools in southern VA are are typically inferior to those in northern VA in terms of physical attributes and opportunities. Growing up in SOVA (southern VA), we were always told how we would never be able to compete with kids from NOVA when it came time for college applications, etc. I could not find data for public education funding, but what I ended up using was the number of residents per county of the age 25 or older who obtained a Bachelor's degree or higher. I was hoping this would relate by showing that there was a higher concentration in NOVA, but as you can see there are very few data points throughout Virginia except for the Washington D.C. area.
