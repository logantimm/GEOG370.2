Chloropleth of National Parks in the U.S.

Logan Timm

I love our nation's beautiful parks and reserves. For this project, I simplified the dataset to only look at only National Parks rather than look at all federally maintained natural areas. I wanted to see which states had more area of natural park land than others, so if you could only choose a few states to visit you could make the most of your time.


The following map is a chloropleth of the cumulative area of national parks in each state. As you can see, Alaska clearly has significantly more area than any other state.



The second map is done excluding Alaska from the dataset to see a more accurate representation of the differences between states.
